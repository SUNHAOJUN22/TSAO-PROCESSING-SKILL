# Qualification boundary

## Software artifact

The TSAO source tree, schemas, CLI and tests may be qualified by repository CI and deterministic release checks.

## Not automatically qualified

The following remain `NOT_EVALUATED` until real project evidence and named accountable review are recorded:

- catalyst synthesis, analytical characterization and high-pressure experiments;
- thermodynamic, kinetic, CFD or commercial simulator models for a specific design;
- equipment mechanical design and relief-system sizing;
- HAZOP, LOPA, SIL, environmental permit and regulatory approval;
- freedom-to-operate legal opinion;
- customer compound, conversion-line and part durability qualification;
- pilot, demonstration, commissioning and industrial performance guarantee.

A green CI run, generated report, populated template or converged simulation must never be represented as one of these approvals.
