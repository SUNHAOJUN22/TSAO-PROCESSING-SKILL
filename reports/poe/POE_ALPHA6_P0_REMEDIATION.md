## False-PASS closure

The former eight-file/non-empty audit is removed. The current auditor fails or holds on:

- placeholder or content-free deliverables;
- unsafe or traversing paths;
- non-object manifest records;
- missing/duplicate groups, IDs or paths;
- hash and byte-count mismatch;
- malformed property/process records;
- invalid compositions, equipment references or tolerances;
- unclosed recycle and missing dynamic assets;
- empty requirement evidence, open conflicts and missing named approval.

Rich Chinese legacy packages are mapped rather than reported as wholly missing, but remain HOLD until normalized and cross-referenced.
