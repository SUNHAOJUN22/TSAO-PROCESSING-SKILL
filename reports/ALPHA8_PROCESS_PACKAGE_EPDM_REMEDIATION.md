# Alpha.8 universal process-package and EPDM remediation

Alpha.8 closes two structural gaps: process-general now has a reusable executable process-package model rather than only prose modules, and EPDM is upgraded from two documents to a flagship runtime with fourteen module contracts, twenty requirements, reference kernels, Schema, fixtures, CLI, wheel delivery and adversarial tests.

The implementation deliberately does not invent an EPDM historical asset registry. Historical experiments, commercial simulator files, catalyst synthesis, HSE studies, compounds and customer trials remain external and `NOT_EVALUATED`.
