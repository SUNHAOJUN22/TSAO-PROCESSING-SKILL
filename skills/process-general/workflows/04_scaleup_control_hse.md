# 04 Scaleup Control Hse

## Purpose

Establish scale claims, pilot learning, dynamic operability, safeguards, reliability and environmental closure.

## Inputs

Qualified upstream Gate artifacts, named owner, evidence IDs, unresolved assumptions and acceptance criteria.

## Steps

1. Verify input status and fail closed on missing provenance.
2. Execute or schedule each calculation, experiment and independent review.
3. Record observed, calculated, inferred, proposed and approved states separately.
4. Publish reusable machine-readable artifacts and a concise decision memo.
5. Audit conservation, applicability, uncertainty, safety boundary and cross-file links.

## Exit

The workflow exits only with explicit evidence, decision, owner, residual risk and next Gate. Unexecuted external work remains `REQUIRES_EXTERNAL_EXECUTION`.
