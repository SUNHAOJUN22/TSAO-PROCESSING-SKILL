# Control and operability

**Decision:** Can the process remain within quality, equipment and safety constraints during normal operation, disturbances and transitions?

**Gate interface:** G8–G17. Define controlled/manipulated/disturbance variables, constraints, observability, alarms, interlocks, transitions and operator actions.

**Required work:** derive dynamics from a qualified physical basis; perform controllability/observability and sensor-latency checks; test feed, utility, fouling, analyser and equipment disturbances; separate regulatory control, optimization and independent protection layers; define start-up/shutdown and off-spec routing.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when steady-state convergence is mistaken for operability, critical states are unobservable or protection depends on the same failure cause.

**Outputs:** control narrative, cause-and-effect, alarm/interlock basis, disturbance matrix, transition plan, operator response and dynamic validation record.

**External accountable work:** control-system implementation, alarm rationalization, SIS lifecycle work, operator validation and site acceptance require named control, safety and operations authorities.
