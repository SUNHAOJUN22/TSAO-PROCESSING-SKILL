# Measurement and data qualification

**Decision:** Are sampling, analytical and data systems capable of supporting the intended Gate decision?

**Gate interface:** G3–G7. Define sampling boundary, sample stability, calibration, bias, precision, detection/quantification limits, time alignment, missing-data rules and data lineage.

**Required work:** create material/sample/method/instrument/dataset passports; run GR&R and uncertainty budgets; detect gross errors before reconciliation; preserve transformations, exclusions and deviations; use guardbands for acceptance.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when method capability, traceability, sample stability, units or time alignment are unknown.

**Outputs:** measurement plan, method qualification, calibration/GR&R report, data dictionary, reconciliation basis and acceptance uncertainty statement.

**External accountable work:** accredited laboratories, method owners and plant sampling authorities must execute and sign calibration, validation, chain-of-custody and regulatory acceptance activities.
