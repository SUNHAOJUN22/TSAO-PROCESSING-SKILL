# Separation, recycle and finishing

**Decision:** Can the process meet product purity, recovery, residuals, waste and utility targets across the full recycle network?

**Gate interface:** G9–G13. Evaluate distillation, absorption, extraction, adsorption, membranes, reactive separation, crystallization, filtration, centrifugation and drying.

**Required work:** close component and impurity balances; include inerts, light/heavy by-products, purge and off-spec disposition; qualify non-ideal and non-equilibrium models; quantify product losses, solvent/water/VOC and energy; test recycle accumulation and transient inventory.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when purge, trace impurity, phase split, residual or waste treatment is missing or an equilibrium stage model is used outside its domain.

**Outputs:** separation basis, recycle network, purge optimization, residual specification, utility/waste inventory and equipment/test requirements.

**External accountable work:** pilot separation trials, hazardous material handling, waste-treatment acceptance and equipment guarantees require qualified laboratories, vendors and responsible engineers.
