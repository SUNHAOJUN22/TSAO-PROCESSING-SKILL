# TEA, LCA, supply chain and IP interface

**Decision:** Is the preferred route viable and resilient under uncertainty without hiding boundary, allocation or supply assumptions?

**Gate interface:** G2–G18. Define capacity, yield, raw materials, utilities, labour, capital, waste, emissions, logistics, supplier qualification and IP/legal handoffs.

**Required work:** align technical and economic cases; perform sensitivity/scenario and break-even analysis; declare LCA boundary and allocation; test feed/energy/carbon/price/schedule uncertainty; identify single-source and critical-material risks; prepare claim charts and FTO handoff without legal conclusions.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when economics uses unqualified yields, LCA omits major inventories, or supply/IP risks lack accountable actions.

**Outputs:** TEA model, uncertainty tornado/scenarios, LCA inventory, supply-risk register, sourcing qualification and FTO counsel package.
