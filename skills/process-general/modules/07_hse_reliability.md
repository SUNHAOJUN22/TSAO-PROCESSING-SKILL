# HSE, mechanical integrity and reliability

**Decision:** Are hazards, protection layers, materials integrity and availability requirements sufficiently defined for the next Gate?

**Gate interface:** G6–G18. Cover inventories, reaction/runaway, toxic/flammable release, dust, corrosion/erosion, fouling/plugging, rotating equipment, inspection, maintainability and critical spares.

**Required work:** prepare HAZID/HAZOP/LOPA/SIL and relief handoffs without claiming external completion; test common-cause and utility failures; quantify availability and proof-test assumptions; link barriers to evidence, ownership and degradation mechanisms.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when a credible scenario lacks detection, mitigation, independent review or a maintained barrier.

**Outputs:** hazard register, safeguard/bow-tie map, integrity and inspection basis, RAM model, critical-spares plan and external safety-study handoff.
