# Bioprocess overlay

**Decision:** Can the biological state and downstream process deliver reproducible titre, yield, quality and contamination control at scale?

**Gate interface:** G3–G18. Define organism/enzyme state, medium, sterility, inhibition, oxygen demand, broth rheology, heat generation, foaming, contamination and downstream recovery.

**Required work:** close carbon/redox and yield balances; quantify OUR/OTR and kLa with scale dependence; distinguish growth, maintenance and production kinetics; qualify sampling and viable-state measurements; test mixing, gas transfer, sterilization and hold-time limits.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when biological state, contamination strategy, oxygen-transfer margin or downstream recovery basis is unqualified.

**Outputs:** bioprocess basis, media/feed strategy, mass-transfer/heat model, contamination controls, harvest/downstream plan and biosafety handoff.

**External accountable work:** strain authorization, biosafety, sterility validation, viable-state testing, GMP/regulatory acceptance and pilot fermentation require qualified biological laboratories and accountable site authorities.
