# Solids and crystallization overlay

**Decision:** Can solid form, particle-size distribution and downstream handling be controlled reproducibly across scale?

**Gate interface:** G3–G18. Cover supersaturation, nucleation, growth, agglomeration, breakage, polymorph, seeding, slurry rheology, filtration, washing, drying, dust and electrostatics.

**Required work:** qualify solubility and metastable-zone data; construct PBM/PSD hypotheses; test seed history, mixing and cooling/antisolvent trajectories; link particle attributes to filtration/drying and product performance; define dust and static controls.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when polymorph, supersaturation, PSD measurement or slurry handling is unqualified.

**Outputs:** crystallization/precipitation basis, PBM/PSD model, seed and trajectory strategy, solid-liquid separation plan, drying/handling basis and acceptance tests.
