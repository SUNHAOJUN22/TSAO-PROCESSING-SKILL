# Electrochemical overlay

**Decision:** Can electrochemical performance, selectivity, degradation and thermal/gas management meet the process and product requirements?

**Gate interface:** G3–G18. Cover charge balance, current efficiency, electrode kinetics, ohmic loss, mass transfer, gas evolution, electrolyte/speciation, heat and component ageing.

**Required work:** calculate Faradaic efficiency and voltage breakdown; separate kinetic, ohmic and concentration polarization; qualify electrode area and flow distribution; test gas disengagement, crossover, impurities and degradation; define electrical isolation and emergency response.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when charge balance, product accounting, cell heat or degradation is unresolved.

**Outputs:** cell/process model, current/energy efficiency, material compatibility, gas/thermal basis, lifetime test and scale-up/stack qualification plan.
