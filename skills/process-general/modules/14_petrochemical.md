# Petrochemical and refining overlay

**Decision:** Can feed variability, catalyst life, fractionation, high-pressure integrity and utility networks meet product and availability requirements?

**Gate interface:** G3–G18. Cover feed envelope, catalyst deactivation, coke/fouling, hydrogen and steam balance, high-temperature/high-pressure materials, fractionation constraints, flare and heat integration.

**Required work:** qualify feed assays and contaminants; model catalyst activity/selectivity and regeneration; close fractionator and recycle constraints; evaluate corrosion and integrity windows; test hydrogen/steam/power/cooling/flare disturbances; avoid heat integration that removes operability or independent protection.

**Fail closed:** HOLD when feed envelope, catalyst state, integrity, fractionation bottleneck or flare/utility capacity is unqualified.

**Outputs:** feed/catalyst basis, process and fractionation model, utility/flare balance, integrity/corrosion plan, debottleneck cases and performance-test protocol.
