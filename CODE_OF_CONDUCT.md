# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We commit to a harassment-free, evidence-respecting and professionally accountable community. Be respectful, distinguish critique of claims from critique of people, disclose conflicts of interest, protect confidential process information and correct errors transparently.

Unacceptable conduct includes harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, falsification of evidence, deliberate concealment of safety or integrity issues, and misrepresentation of software output as real technical approval.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Maintainers may edit, reject or remove contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect contributors, users and public safety.
