# Change-impact propagation

A change in requirement, material, catalyst, method, model, equipment, control, regulation or customer condition must propagate through the typed digital thread.

The impact assessment identifies affected evidence, datasets, parameters, models, balances, process documents, equipment, controls, barriers, qualification tests, Gates and releases. Each affected object receives one of: no impact with justification, document update, recalibration, selective requalification, full requalification or retirement.

A previous PASS is reopened when its supporting path is invalidated. Change control may not rely solely on document revision history; it must evaluate the scientific and engineering dependency graph.
