# Experiment-design contract

Experiments are selected for decision value, not convenience alone. A proposed experiment records hypothesis, competing explanation, controlled factors, nuisance factors, measurement method, randomization/blocking, replication, sample-size rationale, safety boundary, expected information gain, model parameter or Gate affected, analysis plan, stop rule and data passport.

A design must be capable of falsifying at least one meaningful hypothesis. Repeated measurements are not independent process replicates. Missing measurement capability, unsafe conditions, confounded factors or an analysis plan written after observing results block execution or interpretation.
