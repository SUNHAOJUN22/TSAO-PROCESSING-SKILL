# Uncertainty policy

TSAO separates measurement, parameter, model-form, numerical, scale-up, scenario and decision uncertainty. Each uncertainty source is quantified where practical or explicitly bounded and ranked when quantification is not defensible.

Uncertainty is propagated to the decision variable and acceptance criterion. A narrow fitted confidence interval does not cover model-form error or scale mismatch. High-consequence decisions require sensitivity to plausible alternatives and independent evidence.

Uncertainty is not erased by averaging, optimization or a converged solver. Report the range, dominant contributors, applicability domain, risk treatment and evidence needed to reduce it.
