# Process technology-package index

A mature package may include, as applicable:

1. design basis and product/application requirements;
2. chemistry, catalyst, reaction mechanism and kinetic basis;
3. analytical, raw-material and data-quality basis;
4. thermodynamic and physical-property method qualification;
5. PFDs, stream tables and material/energy balances;
6. equipment, utility, relief and emissions design bases;
7. P&ID, control, alarm, interlock and operating narratives;
8. separation, recycle, impurity, purge and finishing basis;
9. laboratory, bench, pilot, demonstration and scale-up reports;
10. model dossiers, uncertainty and applicability domains;
11. HAZID/HAZOP/LOPA/SIL and mechanical-integrity handoffs;
12. environmental, TEA, LCA, supply-chain and IP/FTO handoffs;
13. product, customer and regulatory qualification;
14. commissioning, performance-test, training and transfer plans;
15. requirement-to-evidence-to-test acceptance matrix;
16. change control, field monitoring and continuous-validation plan.

The index is tailored to the project. Presence of a heading or template is not proof of technical completion.
