# Why TSAO

Chemical-process development commonly fails at interfaces: literature claims are not tied to design conditions, laboratory data lack measurement boundaries, fitted kinetics are not identifiable, scale-up preserves the wrong physics, simulations become the sole source of truth, and generated documents are mistaken for approvals.

TSAO addresses these failures with a typed digital thread, fail-closed Gates, model-risk qualification, specialist inheritance, software-neutral contracts and deterministic release evidence. Its goal is not maximum autonomous prose. Its goal is a project in which decisions, assumptions, evidence, calculations, experiments, equipment and acceptance tests can be followed and challenged.
