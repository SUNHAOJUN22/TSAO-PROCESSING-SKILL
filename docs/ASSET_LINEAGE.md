## External methodology influences

The project independently implements ideas influenced by:

- GPT Researcher: planner/executor/publisher separation, parallel evidence acquisition and source tracking;
- Scientific Agent Skills: modular executable scientific capabilities and tool routing;
- Nature Skills: reusable verified workflows and directly usable research artifacts;
- Academic Research Skills: human-in-the-loop integrity gates, material passports, independent review and reproducibility controls;
- open process-systems engineering ecosystems such as DWSIM/CAPE-OPEN, IDAES, Cantera, Pyomo, CoolProp and FMI.

Each external project retains its own licence. CC BY-NC material is methodology-only and is not redistributed. No external method, paper, patent or web parameter becomes a design or production set point without project-specific qualification.

## Status inheritance rule

Historical evidence and models may be imported, but technical approval never is. Every new project begins with:

```text
scientific_approval: NOT_EVALUATED
engineering_approval: NOT_EVALUATED
hse_approval: NOT_EVALUATED
fto_approval: NOT_EVALUATED
customer_approval: NOT_EVALUATED
industrial_performance_guarantee: NOT_EVALUATED
```

A Gate can advance only with scoped evidence, acceptance criteria, named approvers, traceable decisions and passed machine/human checks.
