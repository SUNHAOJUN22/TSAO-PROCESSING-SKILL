# Research and engineering integrity

TSAO labels statements as verified fact, supported inference, assumption, proposal, calculated result, experimental result or approval. These classes must not be silently converted into one another.

## Source integrity

- Cite claim-level locators rather than a document title alone.
- Record source, publication/effective date, retrieval date, evidence grade, applicability, contradiction status and review expiry.
- Retracted, superseded, expired or unresolved contradictory evidence cannot support a PASS decision without explicit resolution.
- Patents establish disclosure and claims, not commercial truth or freedom to operate.

## Data integrity

- Preserve raw data and immutable provenance.
- Record units, sampling boundary, instrument, calibration, analyst, preprocessing and exclusions.
- Do not treat values below detection limits as zero without a declared censoring method.
- Prevent train/validation leakage and repeated-use optimism.

## Model integrity

- State the decision purpose and model-risk class.
- Test conservation, dimensions, known solutions, limits, sensitivity, identifiability and applicability domain.
- Validate high-risk models independently and retain rejected alternatives and failure evidence.

## Human accountability

AI may organize evidence, draft protocols and run software tests. Named qualified humans remain accountable for experimental execution, engineering approval, process safety, legal opinion, customer acceptance and industrial performance.
