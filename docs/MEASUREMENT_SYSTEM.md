# Measurement-system qualification

Before a measurement supports kinetics, product specification, model calibration or a Gate, qualify selectivity, calibration range, traceability, repeatability, reproducibility, bias, linearity, stability, detection/quantitation limits, sampling and preparation, instrument-to-instrument correlation and uncertainty.

A precise method may still be biased or non-representative. Different standards or instrument designs may not be interchangeable. Method changes trigger impact assessment and, when necessary, bridge studies and selective requalification.
