# Independent review

Independent review is proportionate to consequence. MR4/MR5 models, safety-critical barriers, customer-critical qualification and performance-guarantee bases require a reviewer who did not author the primary work and who has relevant competence.

The reviewer receives the decision question, frozen evidence set, model/data passport, known limitations, failed alternatives and acceptance criteria. The review records agreement, divergence, requested tests, residual risk and signed disposition. Reviewer absence is a blocking defect, not a formatting omission.
